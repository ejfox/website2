For [Logically](https://www.logically.ai/), I produced data visualizations for investigations into online disinformation movements.

## The Rise of Qlobal Change & Querdenken_711

A force-directed map of the Telegram universe around Querdenken_711 — the channel at the center of Germany's "Querdenken" anti-lockdown movement — paired with an audience-growth timeline keyed to the start of the pandemic, the August 2020 protests, and the January 6th US Capitol insurgency. (Reporting: Jordan Wildon / Logically.)
