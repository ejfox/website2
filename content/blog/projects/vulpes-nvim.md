A cyberpunk neon colorscheme for Neovim — signature vulpes pink, teal comments, dark and light variants, and monthly color rotations. Ships with matching themes for the rest of the terminal: wezterm, kitty, ghostty, alacritty, tmux, lazygit, bat, and fzf.
