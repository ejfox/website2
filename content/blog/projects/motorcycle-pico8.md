A Pico-8 "video game poem" about the feeling of riding a motorcycle, with speed-reactive perlin-wave road sway and a music engine that escalates through five patterns as speed climbs, then collapses on a crash. EJ's brother made the sprites and tracker music; Coach Artie, EJ's AI assistant, did most of the coding.
