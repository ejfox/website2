## OUTLINE

### The $5K visualization

- Works in the demo
- Breaks with real data
- No documentation
- Developer disappears after launch
- You own the bugs forever

### The $50K visualization

- Works in production
- Handles edge cases
- Documented for your team
- Stress-tested before launch
- I'm around for launch support

### What you're actually paying for

Not my time. You're paying for:

1. **10 years of mistakes I've already made** (so you don't have to)
2. **Relationships with data providers** (I know who to call)
3. **Pattern recognition** (I've seen this failure mode before)
4. **Reputation stake** (my name is on it too)

### When to hire cheap

- Internal dashboards no one will see
- Prototypes that will be thrown away
- Projects with zero stakes

### When to hire expensive

- Anything going on TV
- Anything with legal/regulatory implications
- Anything that will be cited as a source
- Anything where failure is public

### The real question

Not "can we afford $50K?" but "can we afford to fail?"
